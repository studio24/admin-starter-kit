About this example
  • The note should only appear after the form has been submitted. It's loaded into div id="site-aria-status". Notes are injected into these empty divs when needed, which is the most robust method for making sure screen reader users are aware of the additional content.
  • The note message is deliberately vague: it must not expose whether a username exists
  • The novalidate attribute on the form turns off HTML5 in-browser validation, which is not very accessible
